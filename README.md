## 📌 Project Overview  
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on global satellite data.  
The goal is to analyze satellite growth, orbital congestion, and space sustainability by combining multiple datasets and building interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  

- Clean and preprocess satellite datasets  
- Merge data from multiple sources (APIs + datasets)  
- Analyze satellite launch trends over time  
- Identify key countries and operators  
- Study orbital distribution and congestion  
- Visualize insights using dashboards  

---

## 📁 Dataset  

The dataset includes information such as:

- Satellite name  
- Country of origin  
- Operator  
- Launch year  
- Orbit type (LEO, MEO, GEO, Elliptical)  
- Purpose (communication, military, etc.)  
- Satellite mass and altitude  

**Sources:**
- Kaggle UCS Satellite Dataset  
- Celestrak API  
- SpaceTrack API

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed  

1. Satellite Growth Trends  
Analyze yearly satellite launches and identify growth patterns.  
Understand how satellite deployment accelerated after 2019.  

2. Country & Operator Analysis  
Identify top countries contributing to satellite launches.  
Analyze dominant operators (private vs government).  

3. Orbit Distribution  
Evaluate which orbits are most crowded (LEO, GEO, etc.).  
Understand risks associated with orbital congestion.  

4. Purpose-Based Analysis  
Analyze satellite usage (communication, military, earth observation).  
Determine dominant sectors.  

5. Space Debris & Sustainability  
Study growth of debris, rocket bodies, and inactive satellites.  
Assess long-term risks for space missions.

## 📈 Insights  

- Satellite launches have grown rapidly, especially after 2019  
- Low Earth Orbit (LEO) is the most crowded region  
- Private companies dominate satellite operations  
- Communication satellites form the largest category  
- Space debris is increasing and poses serious risks  

1. Growth Surge – Rapid increase driven by commercial players  
2. Orbit Congestion – LEO is highly saturated  
3. Market Dominance – Few operators control majority share  
4. Purpose Trends – Communication dominates usage  
5. Sustainability Risk – Rising debris threatens future missions
